Welcome to the 1.0 beta version of Modernizer. The easy to use css Framework built on the basis of elements to be realized through built-in classes.

Designing simple, flexible and efficient designs becomes a breeze!
Use the various components offered to build your sites very quickly and with a great customization.

Forget about painful frameworks that force you to rewrite all the properties of added classes that you don't want and easily add functionality without the headache.

Carefully designed with the goal of offering its users a free, modern-oriented tool with lots of possibilities.
